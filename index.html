Our History

CSHS is an organization, founded by students, for students, and of students.

The Travis Computer Science Honor Society was founded in 2020 in order to create access to enrichment, competition, and challenge opportunities for current Computer Science Students at Travis High School.

Our Mission

The Travis High School Computer Science Honor Society promotes Computer Science enrichment through contests and academic development, catered towards dedicated CS students, creating a collaborative community where students can be inspired and equipped to pursue futures beyond what they can imagine.
